# Quality Inspection

In ERPNext, you can mark your incoming or outgoing products for Quality
Inspection. To enable ERPNext to perform this function, go to:

### 1. How to create a new Packing Slip
1. Go to: **Stock > Tools > Quality Inspection > New**.
1. Select the inspection type whether incoming, outgoing, or in process.
1. Select the Item and set the sample size which will be inspected.
1. You can change who it's inspected by and also add who it's verified by.
1. Save and Submit.
