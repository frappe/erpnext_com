Speaking at the conference

Key ERPNext users will share their experiences, tips and ideas for taking your business planning online. Developers will showcase the apps they've built on the Frappe platform.

Jordan Hubbard

CTO iX Systems and Co-founder of the FreeBSD Project

Jordan was the Director of Unix at Apple and helped put Unix in the heart of millions of devices. He will talk on how open source can be used to build commercial businesses.

Rushabh Mehta

Founder of ERPNext and the Frappe Framework

Rushabh will share some of the latest developments by the ERPNext team and the roadmap for putting ERPNext at the heart of thousands of businesses across the world.

Abhay Singh

Designer at Studio March

Studio March is responsible for the new look and feel of ERPNext and the Frappe Framework. Abhay will talk about the thought and creative process behind the first design-focused update to ERPNext.

Also speaking at the conference:
Kovid Goyal, the creator of the popular E-book converter Calibre,
talks by users and developers of ERPNext.
